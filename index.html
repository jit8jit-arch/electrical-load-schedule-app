Engineering notes

Built around attached references

  • DEWA Appendix 5 tables are used as the base for cable and breaker sizing logic.
  • ABB enclosed switch-disconnector models are used for automatic isolator recommendations.
  • Attached Oman Cables and Nexans data are used for outer diameter, voltage drop and accessory guidance where available.
  • Fault level, correction factors, special motor starting, ambient derating and final coordination still need engineer review before issue.
